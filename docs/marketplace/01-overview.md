# Feature-Reacher

## Surface Underutilized Features Before They Become Technical Debt

---

### What Is Feature-Reacher?

Feature-Reacher is a documentation analysis tool that identifies potential feature adoption risks in your product. It analyzes release notes, help center articles, and FAQs to surface features that may be underutilized—backed by evidence, not guesswork.

---

### Key Benefits

**No Analytics Required**
Don't have usage data? No problem. Feature-Reacher works with what you already have: product documentation.

**Evidence-Backed Insights**
Every diagnosis includes cited evidence from your documents. Trace any finding back to its source.

**Repeatable Audits**
Save artifact sets and compare audits over time to track improvement.

**Stakeholder-Ready Reports**
Export professional summaries for leadership and partners.

---

### How It Works

1. **Upload Your Docs**
   Paste release notes, documentation, or FAQs into Feature-Reacher.

2. **Run the Audit**
   The analysis engine extracts features, scores visibility, and generates diagnoses.

3. **Act on Insights**
   Review ranked features, understand risks, and export actionable reports.

---

### Who It's For

- **Product Managers** seeking to improve feature discovery
- **Documentation Teams** optimizing help content
- **Technical Writers** identifying documentation gaps
- **Customer Success** understanding feature adoption patterns
