Acme Analytics - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I install the tracking code?
A: Copy the tracking snippet from Settings > Install and paste it before the closing </head> tag on every page you want to track.

Q: Can I track multiple websites?
A: Yes! With Multi-property Support, you can manage unlimited websites from one account. Each property gets its own tracking ID.

Q: How often does data update?
A: Standard reports update every hour. Real-time Dashboard updates every 5 seconds for live visitor data.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes, you can export to CSV, Excel, or PDF. For programmatic access, use our API with JSON responses.

Q: What is Funnel Analysis?
A: Funnel Analysis lets you track how users move through a series of steps (like signup > onboarding > first action) and identify where they drop off.

Q: How do Scheduled Reports work?
A: Set up automated reports that deliver to your inbox on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis. Configure which metrics, date ranges, and recipients in Reports > Scheduled.

Q: What are Custom Alerts?
A: Alerts notify you when metrics cross thresholds you define. For example, get notified when bounce rate exceeds 50% or when daily signups drop below average.

Q: Can I share dashboards with my team?
A: Yes! Use Team Workspaces to invite members with role-based access. You can also create Public Dashboards that anyone with the link can view (read-only).

Q: What is Session Replay?
A: Session Replay records user sessions so you can watch exactly how visitors interact with your site. Note: sensitive fields are automatically masked.

Q: How do Heatmaps work?
A: Heatmaps visualize aggregate click and scroll data, showing you which areas of your pages get the most engagement.

Q: What integrations are available?
A: We support Webhook Integrations for pushing data to external systems, plus native integrations with popular A/B Testing platforms.

Q: How long is data retained?
A: Default retention is 24 months. Enterprise plans include Data Retention Policies to customize this based on your compliance needs.

Q: Is there an API?
A: Yes, our REST API provides programmatic access to all your data. Check the API Rate Limiting Dashboard to monitor your usage.

Q: Do you support SSO?
A: Yes, Enterprise plans include SAML 2.0 SSO integration.

Q: What is Cohort Analysis?
A: Cohort Analysis groups users by a shared characteristic (like signup week) and tracks their behavior over time to identify trends.

Q: Can I annotate my charts?
A: Yes! Use Annotations to mark important events (like product launches or marketing campaigns) directly on your charts for context.

Q: What are Custom Dimensions?
A: Custom Dimensions let you define your own tracking attributes beyond the standard ones, like user plan type or feature flags.

Q: How do Advanced Segments work?
A: Create complex user segments using AND/OR logic to filter your data by any combination of properties and behaviors.
